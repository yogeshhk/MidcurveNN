Yogesh Kulkarni (yogeshkulkarni@yahoo.com) is the author of MidcurveNN — a research project computing midcurves (medial axis/skeleton) of 2D polygons using neural networks. This is his PhD research topic. He has:
- Published papers at CAD Conference 2021, CAD & Applications journal 2022, viXra 2019
- Given talks at ODSC India 2019
- Kaggle datasets and notebooks for this project
- Strong background in geometric deep learning, graph algorithms, and CAD geometry
- Familiar with TensorFlow/Keras, PyTorch, HuggingFace, and torch-geometric
